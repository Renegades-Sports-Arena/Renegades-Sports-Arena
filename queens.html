ABOUT RENEGADES QUEENS

Renegades Queens is built on the pillars of power, technique, and strategic brilliance. We are not just a training camp; we are a dedicated franchise ecosystem committed to bringing women's cricket in Karnataka to international standards.

Our panel delivers professional coaching, KSCA division integrations, digital video analytics, and fitness regimes custom-designed for the modern female athlete.
